üç¶ Ice Cream Customer Analysis

Understanding customer preferences to improve flavors, pricing, and marketing

üìå Overview

This project analyzes customer preferences for ice cream flavors using a structured data lifecycle approach. The goal is to help the company understand trends and improve decision-making using real customer data.

üìä Data Lifecycle Process

1 Plan: Defining the Data Needs

  • Date & Time: When the ice cream was purchased.
  • Flavor: Type of ice cream bought.
  • Price: Cost of the ice cream.
  • Reason: Optional customer feedback.
  • Customer Age: Analyze preferences by age group.
  • Customer Location: Latitude and longitude.
  • Rating: Customer rating (1-5).

Expected Insights: Popular flavors by season, age, location; price impact on sales; customer satisfaction trends.

2 Capture: Collecting the Data

  • Data collected at point of sale.
  • Customers optionally provide details.
  • Store location used to retrieve coordinates.

3 Manage: Storing and Securing Data

  • Data stored in MySQL for structured queries.
  • Secure access controls for sensitive info.
  • Regular backups maintained.

4 Analyze: Extracting Insights

Tools Used: MySQL, Tableau

  • Popular flavors by age group.
  • Sales trends over time, season, and location.
  • Customer satisfaction ratings.
  • Heatmap of purchases by region.

5 Archive: Managing Old Data

  • Data older than 3 years archived for historical reference.
  • Summarized reports generated.
  • Older datasets stored on separate hard drives.

6 Destroy: Removing Unnecessary Data

  • Delete data no longer needed.
  • Anonymize personal details before deletion.
  • Use secure data wiping methods for compliance.
